The product is a Pure Evoke H4 radio. It was released in 2016. The radio has a wooden finish, available in oak or walnut, and features a full-color 2.4-inch TFT display. It functions as an FM/DAB/DAB+ radio and a Bluetooth speaker. Other features include an Aux-in port, a 10-watt speaker, dual alarms with snooze, kitchen and sleep timers, and the ability to save 40 preset stations with 6 dedicated buttons. It can be powered by mains or optionally with a ChargePAK F1 battery pack.
