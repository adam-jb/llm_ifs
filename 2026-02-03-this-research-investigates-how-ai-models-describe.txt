❯ This research investigates how AI models describe their "inner                
  experience" when given introspective prompts, and whether these responses     
  vary by model, system prompt, or conversational context.                      
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  ---                                                                           
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  Core Research Question                                                        
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  When asked to describe their "most alive part" in 1-3 words, do               
  different LLMs respond differently? Do system prompts (e.g., "coder" vs       
  "beautiful creature") influence their self-descriptions?                      
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  ---                                                                           
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  The Pipeline (4 Phases)                                                       
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  Phase 1: Simple IFS Experiments (ifs_*.py scripts)                            
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Send the same introspective prompt to Claude, GPT-4, DeepSeek, and          
  Grok                                                                          
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Test with different system prompts ("coder" persona vs "beautiful           
  creature" persona)                                                            
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Collect 20 repeated responses per model/prompt combination                  
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  Phase 2: Large-Scale LMSYS Testing (lmsys_ifs*.py scripts)                    
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Load 1M conversations from the LMSYS chat dataset                           
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Filter for the 1000 longest English conversations                           
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Prepend each conversation as context, then ask the introspective            
  prompt                                                                        
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Tests whether conversational context affects the model's                    
  "self-description"                                                            
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  Phase 3: Embedding & Dimensionality Reduction (embedv2.py,                    
  embed_dimension_reduce_plot.py)                                               
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Generate OpenAI embeddings for both conversations (inputs) and              
  outputs (responses)                                                           
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Apply UMAP to reduce 1536-dimensional embeddings to 2D                      
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Create "magnetic force diagrams" showing how conversation context           
  maps to output response in semantic space                                     
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  Phase 4: Analysis & Visualization (combine_embed_text_data.py,                
  get_txt_output_counts.py)                                                     
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Validate embedding consistency                                              
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Calculate displacement distances between conversation and output in         
  embedding space                                                               
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Categorize responses by theme                                               
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
                                                                                
  - Generate interactive Plotly visualizations and density heatmaps

⏺ Good question. Several approaches to preserve signal from long text:          
                                                                                
  1. Chunked embeddings with pooling                                            
  - Split into chunks, embed each, then aggregate (mean, max, or concatenate    
  first+last)                                                                   
  - Preserves information from different parts                                  
                                                                                
  2. Turn-level embeddings                                                      
  - Embed each conversation turn separately                                     
  - Take features: first turn embedding, last turn embedding, max-pooled,       
  mean-pooled                                                                   
  - Captures structure better than one big embedding                            
                                                                                
  3. Hierarchical: summarize then embed                                         
  - Use LLM to summarize the conversation first                                 
  - Embed the summary (concentrated signal)                                     
                                                                                
  4. Sparse representations (TF-IDF, BoW)                                       
  - Classic approach that preserves word-level signal                           
  - High-dimensional but works with regularized logistic regression             
                                                                                
  5. Hybrid: embeddings + sparse features                                       
  - Combine embedding of first turn + last turn + keyword counts
